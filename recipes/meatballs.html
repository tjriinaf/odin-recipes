Mom's Spaghetti and Meatballs

These meatballs are really freakin good

Ingredients

  • Three lbs. 80-20 ground beef
  • Two eggs
  • Two cloves mashed garlic
  • 5-6 fistfulls of soaked stale italian bread, water squeezed out
  • about 2/3 cup Locatelli Romano
  • at least 1/2 cup Fresh or fresh frozen ground parsley
  • dash of salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Put beef in large bowl
  2. mix in eggs, preferably by hand
  3. mix in mashed garlic
  4. Remove as must crust as possible from bread, add to bowl and mix
  5. add Locatelli Romano and mix
  6. Add parsley and mix
  7. add a dash of salt and pepper
  8. smell mixture and adjust cheese and parsley until it smells proper sorry, only can describe if you're at my house
