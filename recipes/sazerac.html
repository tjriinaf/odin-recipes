Sazerac, a classic drink.

A classic drink

This classic cocktail is named after the Rye whiskey of which it is usually made with. It is a very old drink, dating back to the speakeasys of New Orleans

The trick with this drink is to coat the inside of the glass with herbsaint only place a drop in, and swirl it around the glass upsidedown. Too much will be overpowering.

The other trick is to twist your lemon over the drink when finished. This step may seem trivial but the oils in the peel impart a valuable flavor.

Ingredients

  • Chilled glass
  • 3 oz rye whiskey. Sazerac rye for authenticity factor
  • half ounce simple syrup or sugar
  • lemon peel

Instructions

  1. Coat glass with herbsaint
  2. Mix ingredients in mixer until ice fully coated in booze
  3. Strain into glass, with boozy ice
  4. Twist lemon peel over drink, and garnish
